Since this episode could be a "snoozer", I keep it lively and share tips and tricks for each app. In the Addendum, I share a first for me and some additional pixel-powered tips and news.

## Show Notes

### Introduction

1. Welcome to episode 0034 and part VI of my six part series on "My Favs." This episode, my favorite Android apps.
2.This could be the most boring episode ever since Android apps are very personal selections and I only install applications I use on a regular basis.
3. I'll share some tips for or highlights for each app to keep it interesting.
4. Your job is to share your favorite apps by commenting on the show post at Reddit.

### Favorite Android Apps

1. I discuss each Android App installed on my Pixel 3 XL.
2. In episode 0029 I shared my favorite Android apps for Chrome OS; these are a bit different.
3. Remember, I'm cheap, err frugal, so I rarely spend money on apps.
4. I'm not a game player.

#### [Adobe Scan][106]

Check out [episode 0011][107] to find out why this app keeps me productive on the go.

#### [Amazon Shopping][109]

Scan barcodes to compare prices and get shipping notifications.

#### [Anchor][111]

My host for the podcast. Record, view stats, and listen to podcasts.

#### [Android Auto][113]

Love hate relationship on my Pixel 3. Love the new UI update.

#### [Calculator][115]

There's really no need to purchase or select and ad supported calculator if you have basic calculator needs.

#### [Calendar][117]

Share calendars with family members. Cools images associated with events such as "lunch."

#### [Camera][119]

The camera app that gives us those wonderful Pixel phone features.

#### [Chrome][121]

Be sure to listen to my [Chrome for Android Flags episode][122].

#### [Clock][124]

Check out timer and stopwatch options. Google Assistant default timer application.

#### [Contacts][126]

Still the easiest contact manager available. Use the check duplicates feature.

#### [Docs][128]

Writing, editing and viewing Google Docs on the go. Now with .docx support.

#### [Drive][130]

100 Gb user here via Google One (later in the show). Use the widget to quickly upload and scan.

#### [Duo][132]

Video chat with family and friends. Try out the new group chats.

#### [Duo Mobile][134]

Not really a "fav" but a required and necessary app.

#### [ExpressVPN][136]

New feature that automatically connects when not on a trusted network. [TRY IT FOR FREE!][137]

#### [Facebook][139]

Like it or not, this is probably where your family is hanging out online.

#### [Feedly][141]

RSS feed reader. Share articles with others.

#### [Files][143]

Find, share, and backup files. Free space on your device.

#### [Fit][145]

Track your steps and your fitness. Link to other fitness apps (Nike Run Club).

#### [FiSwitch][147]

Know which network your riding on and switch between them.

#### [GIF Maker][149]

Make GIFs from videos or photos. GIF screen recorder for Pixel phone.

#### [Gmail][151]

Be sure to use the predictive text feature.

#### [Google Fi][153]

Activation, billing and extras for [Google Fi][154] Subscribers

#### [Google One][156]

Manage personal and family cloud subscription storage and search for offers (I have a 99Â¢ rental available)

#### [Google Wi-Fi][159]

Changed the Wi-Fi converage in my home for the best!

#### [Home][161]

Manage your Google Assistant devices. Explore Routines to get the most features.

#### [Jamboard][163]

You don't need a $5000 Jamboard to Jam. Collaborate online with colleagues.

#### [Keep Notes][165]

Keep and share notes with important information. Listen to [episode 0015][166].

#### [Maps][168]

Local Guide gets you some free goodies.

#### [Messages][170]

Rich Communication Services (RCS) support for enhanced features. Use "Messages for web" to follow messages on your Chromebook or desktop computer.

#### [Nest][172]

Manage Nest camera's and thermostat. Use location awareness to modify temperature when you leave the house.

#### [News][174]

Catchup with news important to you. Spend time telling the app what is relevant to you.

#### [Nike Run Club][176]

Track running, shoe wear, and get inspiration from Coach Bennett.

#### [NoxCleaner][178]

Clean up app caches and search for memory hogging apps. Less and less used on Pixel phones.

#### [Outlook][180]

I have to use Outlook now for work email. Surprise, it's not horrible.

#### [Photos][182]

Love the Assistant. Remember to share albums with family and friends while attending events.

#### [Pocket Casts][184]

The way I listen to podcasts and now free! If you purchased the web version, you have free Plus for life!

#### [Reddit][186]

My newest app and quickly becoming a favorite. Come hang out with us online on the [Pixel Power Podcast subreddit][46].

#### [Rewards][188]

Even when apps cost money, I never pay for them. Answer some questions to earn Google Play credit. My account shows I've earned over $100 in credit.

#### [Sheets][190]

Manage spreadsheets on the go. Remember to tag important files for offline use.

#### [Slides][192]

Create and share electronic presentations on the go. Make last minute touch ups as you prepare for a presentation. Use your phone as a remote. Get Audience questions.

#### [Spotify][194]

Preferred ubiquitous streaming service. Use on Google Home devices.

#### [Teams][196]

Work required Slack competitor. Not so bad and ties to Outlook calendar. Supports Google Docs files.

#### [Tile][198]

Locate physical items. I purchased a [Tile four-pack][199] to get me started and love them. Reverse look up: use tile to find phone too.

#### [Todoist][201]

Manage life and projects. Listen to [episode 0017][202].

#### [Twitter][204]

Get all your tweets. Don't bully and block those who are hateful.

#### [Waze][206]

Navigation at it's finest. Check out the new Google Assistant integration to you can make reports using your voice.

#### [Wyze][208]

I love [Wyze products][209]. Inexpensive, regularly updated, and new smart home products. More in the Addendum.

#### [Xodo][211]

Create and manage PDF files. How is this app free? Learn more in [episode 0013.][212]

#### [YouTube][214]

Get instructional videos for almost ANYTHING!

#### [YouTube TV][216]

Local and national TV on the go! Check out the excellent DVR (shows stored for a year!)

There's long list of applications. Hopefully a few of these will be new and along the way you learned some tips that make your use of the other apps more productive.
